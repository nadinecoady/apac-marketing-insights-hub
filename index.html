Top 5 Emerging Merchant Questions This Quarter

Q1 2026
🤖
"How do I make my products visible to AI agents?"
Agentic Discovery
ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google Business Agent all live or launching in 2026. Shopify's Agentic Storefronts puts products into AI conversations. Google's UCP protocol creates universal standard. Japan webinar on this topic drew 192 RSVPs with zero paid budget. Merchants asking: what data do AI agents need? How do I rank in AI recommendations? Morgan Stanley: ~50% of shoppers will use AI agents by 2030.
🏪
"How do I reduce marketplace dependency without losing volume?"
Marketplace → DTC
APAC merchants on Shopee, Lazada, Rakuten, Tmall want to build DTC but fear losing marketplace volume. CSM analysis found 43 at-risk Plus brands ($91M) and 920 net-new targets. Add-platformers join Shopify for growth, reduced fees, brand control. Most installed apps: ads + marketplaces. Social commerce now ~20% of global ecommerce. SEA + GCR = strongest signal.
🔗
"Can I run online + offline + B2B on one platform?"
Unified Commerce
78% of won APAC deals mention integration. POS/omnichannel theme in 38% of won calls and rising. Japan POS launching Q3 (PayPay, Retail). APAC leads globally in online+offline integration — store sales +20%, non-store +43% projected by 2028. Merchants want one system for DTC, retail, B2B, and international — not 4 platforms. Omnichannel is now operational imperative, not competitive advantage.
🌏
"How do I expand cross-border without managing the complexity?"
Cross-Border Scale
APAC cross-border ecommerce projected to reach $760B GMV by 2026. China/HK export market = $36.55B addressable — larger than Japan + Mexico combined. NIO Firefly chose Shopify Plus specifically for multi-entity expansion stores. FDI into ASEAN +10% as supply chains shift. Merchants want expansion stores, local currencies, local payments — without managing separate tech stacks per market.
⚡
"What does AI actually do for my team today — not the future?"
Practical AI Now
Gap between AI hype and practical value is the #1 feedback from APAC events. Japan webinar attendees: "Show more specifically what Shopify can do, not just the vision." Merchants want: automate busy work (Sidekick), generate product descriptions, personalise at scale, AI-powered search. 48% of won deals mention AI — but lost deals cite "unclear when any of this ships." Gartner: 40% of enterprise apps will include AI agents by 2026, up from 5% in 2025.
